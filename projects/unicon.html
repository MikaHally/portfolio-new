Pitch. Learn. Network.

I’ve gone to my fair share of tech conferences as an attendee during my first semester studying at CODE University of Applied Sciences. There is nothing more exciting than spending time with people who share your passion for technology and product management and listening to inspiring and interesting talks about respective topics. But for me it did not start out that way. Most conferences can be a bit cliquish, composed of subgroups that role in tight circles. I can remember going to conferences and having a hard time getting to know like-minded people. I was wondering if I was the only one who had this problem. Turns out I wasn’t. Moreover, I realized that there was no leading student-led conference on technology and product development. I took on the challenge of changing both and the idea for Unicon was born! I gathered some of my friends and fellow students and started organizing Unicon from the ground up. Our vision was to create a platform for selected students from all around Europe to network and facilitate technical and entrepreneurial learning opportunities in a rapidly evolving tech world by hosting a three day conference in Berlin.

We were able to attract great partners and sponsors that helped us to turn Unicon into reality straight away. Thanks to the large number of student applications that we receive each year we are able to filter out the best talent in tech, design and product through our proven selection process. Companies get access to aspiring developers, designers and product managers and our student talents get new career opportunities going forward. This turned out to be a great success and our sponsors keep coming back year after year.
